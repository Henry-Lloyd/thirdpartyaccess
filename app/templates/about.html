About ThirdParty Access

Where Access Meets Consent — Not another LinkedIn.

Our Mission

ThirdParty Access is dedicated to connecting seekers with very important people (VIPs) and professionals in a secure, transparent, and consent-based manner. Unlike free networking platforms where anyone can message you, we ensure every connection is intentional, verified, and fairly compensated.

Our Vision

To build the most trusted paid-access platform in Malawi and beyond — where professionals monetise their expertise and network, and seekers get genuine, verified connections. Every review is backed by a real payment. Every trust score is earned, not manufactured.

Security & Data Protection

bcrypt Password Hashing

Industry-standard bcrypt with automatic salting protects all passwords.

CSRF Protection

Flask-WTF CSRF tokens on all forms prevent cross-site request forgery.

Rate Limiting

Login endpoints limited to 5 attempts per minute + account lockout after 5 failures.

WAL Mode + Auto Backups

SQLite Write-Ahead Logging for crash safety, with automated backup on every restart.
